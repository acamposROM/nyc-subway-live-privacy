Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 20, 2026

Overview

NYC Subway Live is a real-time subway arrival tracker for New York City. Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains what data we collect and how we use it.

Data Collection

NYC Subway Live does not collect, store, or share any personal data.

Location Data

  • The app requests your location only when you tap the "Use Current Location" button.
  • Your location is used solely to find the nearest subway station on your device.
  • Location data is never sent to any server, stored, or shared with third parties.

Weather Data

  • The app uses Apple WeatherKit to display current weather conditions.
  • Weather requests are made using the station's coordinates, not your personal location.
  • No personal data is shared with weather services.

Station Selection

  • Your selected station is saved locally on your device using UserDefaults so the app remembers your preference between sessions.
  • This data never leaves your device.

Network Requests

  • The app fetches real-time train arrival data directly from the MTA's public GTFS-RT feeds.
  • No personal information is included in these requests.

Third-Party Services

NYC Subway Live does not use any analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or transfer any user data to third parties.

Children's Privacy

The app does not knowingly collect any data from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.

Contact
